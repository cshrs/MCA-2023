Week 7

Revisiting MEI files

This week's task saw us looking at the curational challenges to music. The tasks saw us revisit MEI files and learn how to get the metadata to appear in HTML Verovio pages. This saw an updated version of my MEI file which can be downloaded here, furthermore below is information on the piece including some metadata, styled using CSS. The music can be played using the Verovio JavaScript app, although functionality isn't great.

Music Metadata

Title: Wet Hands
Artist: C418
Arranger: Brian Duffy
Key: F
Tempo: 72
Instrumentation: Piano
Copyright Information: C418's music is protected under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International. The piece 'Wet Hands' has been used as part of the Music Curation and Analytics course through Digital Media and Information Studies at the University of Glasgow and is not to be redistributed. C418 put out his own guidelines on what he will allow to be done with his music, which is linked here.
