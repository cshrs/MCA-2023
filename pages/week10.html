Analysis

As you can see there is a drastic difference in the look of the notation within the piece. However, when I played the newly transcribed music in musescore; I found although it was different, I could still identify the similarity in the music and could easily tell that it was the same track. I noticed changes in the tempo of the track and many notes were changed into chords. Furthermore, the sheet music looks incredibly messsy and would be very difficult for a musician to perform. Overall, I would say this transcription offers little benefit for understanding the music data.
