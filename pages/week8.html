Reflective Statement

In the song "Ancient Stones" from The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim, time-frequency analysis provides a key advantage by revealing details in the music. Specifically, when examining the ethereal strings at the beginning of the track, the spectrogram shows the changing frequencies over time. Unlike a waveform, which captures overall volume changes, the spectrogram identifies the individual frequencies shaping the vocals, horns and strings contained within the track.

This advantage becomes crucial for discerning subtle aspects like vibrato or pitch shifts that might be missed in a waveform. The time-frequency analysis pinpoints when these details occur, offering a deeper understanding of how different frequencies contribute to the overall musical experience.
